## Plan 1 — One-Time Detail (Single Visit, Full Price)

### Description
The One-Time Detail is a standalone appointment with no subscription commitment. The customer pays the standard rate for whichever service package they choose — we come to them, wherever they are in Worcester County. This is the perfect entry point for first-time customers or those who only need an occasional detail.

### Best For
- First-time customers wanting to try the service
- Vehicles needing a one-off deep clean after a specific event (road trip, winter, selling the car)
- Customers with unpredictable schedules who cannot commit to a seasonal plan
- Gift details

### How It Works
- Call or text to schedule at a convenient time and location
- We arrive with all equipment and supplies — no water or power needed from the customer
- Payment is due at time of service
- No contracts, no commitments

## Plan 2 — Silver Plan (2 Details Per Season, 10% Off Each Visit)

### Description
The Silver Plan is our entry-level subscription for customers who want to keep their vehicle well-maintained across two touchpoints in a single season. By committing to two details upfront, the customer receives 10% off the standard rate on both appointments.

Two details in one season allows for a mid-season refresh and an end-of-season clean — removing buildup from one weather period and prepping the vehicle for the next. This plan is ideal for drivers who want regular upkeep without over-scheduling.

### Best For
- Customers who want routine maintenance without over-committing
- Drivers who park outside and deal with seasonal weather, pollen, and road grime
- Anyone who wants to preserve their vehicle's resale value through consistent care
- Customers building an ongoing relationship with their detailer

### How It Works
- At booking, the customer indicates they want the Silver Plan (2 details this season)
- Both appointments are scheduled at their convenience — no requirement to space evenly
- Packages can be mixed and matched between the two visits
- 10% discount is applied automatically at each appointment
- Pay per visit at the discounted rate — no lump sum required upfront
- Both details must be completed within the same season

## Plan Terms & Conditions

- All plans are **seasonal** — appointments must be completed within the same season (approx. 3–4 months)
- Season definitions: Spring (March–May), Summer (June–August), Fall (September–November), Winter (December–February)
- Unused appointments **do not roll over** to the next season
- Appointments can be rescheduled with at least **24 hours notice** at no penalty
- Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may forfeit that appointment's plan discount
- Plans apply to all standard detailing services; ceramic coating and paint correction are eligible but priced individually due to prep time requirements
- **Mix and match** packages between visits is permitted within the same plan
- Plans are **per vehicle** — multiple vehicles require separate plan enrollments
- Pricing ranges reflect vehicle size: compact cars at the lower end, full-size trucks and SUVs at the higher end
